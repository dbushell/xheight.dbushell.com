A Final Word

August 8, 2012
“a blog about ideas and thoughts”

Welcome to the archived xheight — a collaborative blog that ran throughout 2011.

This blog with see no further updates, but it will remain online and accessible in its preserved form. If this is your first visit I’m sure you’ll find something interesting to read. xheight started with no particular agenda other than to provide a platform for designers to explore and share whatever was on their mind. It seems only fitting then that we’re ending in a similar, unplanned fashion; xheight has simply ran its course. Ideas come and go. Interests peak and fade. Whether these articles were successful is entirely subjective. I know I’ve enjoyed xheight. I hope you have too.

Thanks for reading and writing.

Written by David Bushell
